Restaurant & Coffee Shop QR Code Policy

To ensure a seamless, secure, and premium dining experience across all partner venues, MenuQr enforces strict operational standards regarding digital menu accessibility, data collection, and system uptime.

01

Frictionless Guest Access

Scanning a table QR code must direct customers straight to the digital menu via a standard web browser. Venues are strictly prohibited from requiring guests to download third-party applications, sign up, or hand over personal data simply to view items and prices.

02

Customer Data & Privacy

If a restaurant utilizes interactive loyalty or self-ordering features, customer information (such as phone numbers or names) must be processed transparently. Selling, trading, or sharing user analytics with external ad networks is fundamentally banned under our compliance guidelines.

03

Real-Time Pricing & Stock Integrity

Partner venues are responsible for keeping their dynamic dashboards updated. Out-of-stock variations, active promotional sets, and service charges must reflect instantly on the customer's viewport to eliminate order discrepancies and maintain consumer trust.
